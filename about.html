About Day Trader Tools

DayTraderTools.org was created by an active trader who wanted simple, fast tools without clutter.

Many traders waste time switching between apps to check market hours, calculate profit percentages, and track discipline. This site brings those essentials together in one place.

Who This Is For

  • Day traders
  • Swing traders
  • Options traders
  • Stock market learners

The goal is simplicity, speed, and discipline.
